How it Works

A Community built on Sharing

BNBanz .com began operations when friends started their first accommodation directory. For those either on a budget or who may want greater flexibility, than just motel or hotel rooms. For example some travellers may want a B& B, homestay, holiday home, a bach, a beach house, a chalet, an apartment, a villa, or resort, or just a room at a fair price. BNBanz can offer them all.

Trusted Services

BNBanz.com helps make travelling easy, enjoyable and safe. The team check the personal profiles of listings, and maintains a smart messaging system, so that host and guests can communicate with certainty, and manages a trusted platform. The team also reserves the right to cancel any host or guest who does not meet the standards set out by BNBanz.com.

24/7 Customer Support

Whether you are travelling or hosting, BNBanz.com is here for you before during or after your booking experience. Most of the more questions have been answered. See FAQ for the questions and answers.
